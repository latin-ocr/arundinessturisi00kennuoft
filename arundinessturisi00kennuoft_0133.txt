Felices ineat portus, passimque benignas
Misceat aestates Horis Concordia amicis:
‘ Omne quod humanum est' dum tandem regula cuique,
Dum tandem fraternus amor, novus impetus, ultro
Armatas jubeat classes submergier undis,
Ferratasque rui turres :—Natura magistra
Sic doceat gentes cum regibus aequa parare,—
Sic fruges Pax alma beet, sertisque coronet!

E C H O.

Sweet Echo! sweetest nymph, that liv'st unseen
Within thy airy shess,
By slow Meander’s margent green,
And in the violet-embroider'd vale,
Where the love-lorn nightingale
Nightly to thee her sad song mourneth well;
Canst thou not tell me of a gentle pair,
That likest thy Narcissus are?
O! if thou have
Hid them in some flowery cave,
Tell me but where,
Sweet queen of parley, daughter of the sphere ;
So may'st thou be translated to the skies,
And give resounding grace to all Heaven’s hannonies!

M illonus.
