POEMA TA VARJA. 1 2

So steal away with little warning;
Choose thine own time,
Say not ‘good night,’ but in yon happier clime
Bid me ‘good morning.’
Barbauld.

LATINE REDDITL' M.

Vita, diu fuimus comites; et amoena vicissim
Nostra erat, aut moestis nubibus atra, dies.

Tristis amicorum est digressus, nec sine luctu
Scinditur et lachrymis dulce sodalitium.

Tu te ergo tacite subducas; admonitumque
Sit breve, quo libeat tempore abire tibi:

Neu mihi dic ‘valeas,' sed in orbe beatior illo
Alloquere, atque novo mane valere jube.

Lalham.

XXXV.
EPITAPH IN THE ISLE OF WIGHT.

Forgive, blest shade! the tributary tear

That mourns thy exit from a world like this!
Forgive the wish that would have kept thee here,
And stay'd thy progress to the world of bliss!

No more confined to grovelling soenes of night,

No more a tenant pent in mortal clay;

Now should we rather hail thy glorious ssight,

And trace thy passage to the realms of day.
Anon.
